About Me

I'm a Web Developer

I have loved programming for a very long time. I started playing around with databases in the early 2000's during my touring days in the live music industry. I used FileMaker Pro to create my own advance-style database to keep track of each show, venue and promoter that I needed to coordinate logistics. This started my love of the power of databases. I eventually taught myself PHP and learned how to design websites to create a front-end to interface with promoters and venue representatives to start advance work discussions, while connecting to my FileMaker Pro database on the backend (hosted on an old computer I had lying around that I turned into an Apache Server). This eventually lead me to want to learn more about programming. I completed my Associates in Applied Science in Computer Information Technologies with a degree in Programming from Volunteer State Community College. Through this process I further developed my passion in Full-Stack Development. I am fluent in HTML, CSS, Javascript, C#, C++, .NET, Python, SQL, Access and MySQL. After a 30 year career in the live music industry, I have started a new career in web development.

My hobbies are camping and hiking. I am a father to two amazing, grown young men who are both starting their own career paths. I have been married to an amazing, strong woman for the last twenty-four years. We are also parents to two rescue dogs, Leo and Sasha.
